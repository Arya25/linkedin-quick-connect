Customize Your LinkedIn Message

Use this form to set a default message when sending connection requests via Quick Connect.

Just enter your custom message, click on save. Go back to LinkedIn, close the "Add a Note" dialog box and open again.

You have to do this only once

  • firstName will be replaced with the person's first name.
  • cmpny will be replaced with the current company of the person.
Example 1: Hi firstName, I admire your work at cmpny.
Message will become: Hi Shubham, I admire your work at Corp.

Example 2: Hey firstName, let's connect!
Message will become: Hey Shubham, let's connect!

Always preview your message before clicking on Send, always

On selecting the checkbox below, the message will start with "Hi firstName, ".

Your message: I came across your profile and would love to connect.
Message will become: Hi Shubham, I came across your profile and would love to connect.
